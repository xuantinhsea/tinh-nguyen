Research Focus

Quantum Materials

Investigating novel quantum materials with exotic properties for next-generation electronic devices. Our work focuses on topological insulators, superconductors, and quantum dots.

Computational Physics

Developing advanced computational methods for materials simulation using density functional theory (DFT), molecular dynamics, and Monte Carlo techniques.

Machine Learning

Applying machine learning and AI techniques to accelerate materials discovery, predict material properties, and design novel compounds with desired characteristics.
